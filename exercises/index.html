Camp Tecumseh: Where the stars shine bright and the leaves are green. This summer camp in Brookston, Indiana has been creating lasting memories in the hearts of 4–16 year olds for over 50 years.

Camp Tecumseh is a Christian Summer Camp in Brookston, Indiana that is centered around the I'm Third Motto. The I'm Third Motto states: God is First, Others are Second, and I am Third. This motto hopes to instil within campers a dedication to the CACV, which are:

  1. Compassion For All
  2. Compassion for Self
  3. Compassion for Nature

And as is obvious with these values, the Camp does have a religious overtone. Campers begin each day with Chapel and end their day with Devotions where a cabin comes together to share highlights—or lowlights—of their day. Albeit the religious overtone the Camp has no intentions of creating or brainwashing soliders for any denomination, but rather circles around the idea of Red-Lettered Christianity and helping each camper find and be with God in his/her own way. The Camp's Mission is clealy visible in its counselors.
